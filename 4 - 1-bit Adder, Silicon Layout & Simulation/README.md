# Lab 4: 1-bit Adder Silicon Layout and Simulation

This lab applies the primitive-cell work to a complete 1-bit adder implementation. It includes layout, simulation, waveform review, delay measurement, power analysis, and optimization evidence for the supporting inverter, NAND2, and XOR logic.

## Contents

- `Lab 4 Report.pdf` and `Lab 4 Report.docx`: Formal lab report documenting the adder layout, simulations, and results.
- `Pics/`: Supporting screenshots for schematics, simulation waveforms, delay measurements, power analysis, and optimized variants.
- Inverter, NAND2, and XOR waveform captures before and after optimization.
- Delay and power screenshots used to compare original and optimized behavior.

## What This Demonstrates

- Integration of primitive CMOS cells into a functional arithmetic block.
- Simulation-driven verification of logic correctness.
- Delay and power measurement from waveform and simulator output.
- Gate-level optimization based on measured performance.
- Documentation of before-and-after design improvements.

## Review Focus

Review `Lab 4 Report.pdf` first, then inspect the `Pics/` directory for concrete simulation and optimization evidence. This is one of the strongest labs for showing hands-on measurement, iteration, and design tradeoff analysis.
