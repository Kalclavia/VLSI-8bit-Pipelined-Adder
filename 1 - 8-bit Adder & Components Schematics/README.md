# Lab 1: 8-bit Adder and Component Schematics

This lab establishes the schematic-level design foundation for the pipelined 8-bit adder project. It captures the arithmetic and storage components needed for the larger datapath, including full adders, 4-bit adders, 4-bit registers, and the top-level 8-bit pipelined adder structure.

## Contents

- `Lab 1 Report.pdf` and `Lab 1 Report.docx`: Formal lab report documenting the design process and results.
- Full-adder schematic and symbol screenshots.
- 4-bit adder schematic and symbol screenshots.
- 4-bit register schematic, symbol, testbench, and simulation captures.
- 8-bit pipelined adder schematic, symbol, testbench, and simulation captures.

## What This Demonstrates

- Hierarchical schematic capture in Cadence.
- Construction of arithmetic datapath blocks from smaller reusable cells.
- Use of symbols and testbenches to support modular digital design.
- Early functional verification through simulation waveform captures.
- System-level organization of combinational arithmetic and sequential pipeline storage.

## Review Focus

Start with `Lab 1 Report.pdf` for the design narrative, then review the schematic and simulation images to see how the full adder, 4-bit adder, register, and 8-bit pipelined adder are composed and verified.
